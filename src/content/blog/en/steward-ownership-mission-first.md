## Profit follows purpose

Epistola is a steward-owned company. That means no external shareholders, no pressure to maximise growth at your expense, and no exit strategy that turns your dependency into someone else's acquisition play.

Our mission is simple: make document generation for the public sector reliable, affordable, and open. Steward ownership locks that in — legally and permanently.

## What this means for customers

- **Pricing stays fair.** We don't need to extract maximum revenue to satisfy investors.
- **Product decisions serve you.** Features are prioritised by what our customers need, not what a board demands.
- **Long-term commitment.** We're not building to flip. Epistola is designed to be a dependable partner for years to come.

## How it works

Steward ownership separates economic rights from voting rights. The people who run Epistola make the decisions, and profits are reinvested or shared — never extracted by outside capital.
