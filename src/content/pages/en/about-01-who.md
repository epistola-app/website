Epistola is owned and operated by about five people who co-founded the organization. We're not a venture-backed startup chasing growth at all costs—we're a group of founders building software we believe in, together.

We started Epistola because we saw municipalities struggling with document generation tools that were either expensive, inflexible, or locked them into vendor relationships. We wanted to create something better: reliable, open-source software that serves the public good.

Everyone here is a founder in spirit. We make decisions together, we're accountable to each other, and we move forward as a unified team.
