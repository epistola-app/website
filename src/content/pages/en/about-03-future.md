title: "Formalizing our mission with a foundation"
---
Today, our commitment to this mission rests on the values and promises of our founders. But we want to make it permanent. We're working toward creating a foundation (stichting) that will legally guard this mission for the long term.

The foundation will ensure that no future change of ownership or direction can override our core principles: fair pricing, open development, and service to the public good. Until then, we operate by the same principles and personally vouch for them.
